The ~ the ~

The sooner you decide your major, the sooner you will be able to graduate.

早く専攻を決めれば決めるほど早く卒業出来ます。

The more you know, the more you know you don't know.

知れば知るほど、知らないことがあるということを知る。- Aristotle

The more I learn, the more I realize how much I don't know.

学べば学ぶほど、自分がどれだけ知らないかに気づく。- Albert Einstein

The more that you read, the more things you will know.

読めば読むほど、より多くのことを知る。 - Dr. Seuss

as ~ as

Please reply as soon as possible.

出来るだけ早く返信して下さい。

as soon as possibleはASAPと略されることもあります。

I want to spend as much time with my kids as possible.

私は出来るだけ多くの時間を自分の子供と過ごしたいです。

twice as ~ as

We have twice as many employees as we had 9 months ago.

私達（の会社）は９ヶ月前と比べて２倍の従業員を抱えています。

not as ~ as

I am not as smart as him.

私は彼ほど頭が良くありません。(He is smarter than me.)

He doesn't make as many mistakes as he used to.

彼は昔よりもミスをしなくなった。

the same as

Laura is the same age as me.

Lauraは私と同い年です。

Your watch is the same as mine.

あなたの時計は私と同じです。
