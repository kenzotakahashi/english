Driverless Cars

Watch this video and discuess it in your group. It is a free discussion, but the following questions might help you get started.

Questions

How do you feel about the fact that driverless cars can drive better than humans?

When driverless cars become available, would you still drive or use a traditional taxi?

What happens to the jobs of those drivers who are going to be replaced by driverless cars? Do you think automobile companies are responsible for the loss of those jobs?

Do you think driverless cars will be good for society?
